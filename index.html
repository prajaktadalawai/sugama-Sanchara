The Problem We Face

People parking illegally near shops and metro stations causes massive traffic jams. Right now, police only react when a jam happens, and they don't know exactly which parked cars are causing the worst bottlenecks.

How Sugama Sanchara Helps

We use AI to automatically spot illegal parking and calculate exactly how much it's slowing down traffic. Instead of guessing, police can use this dashboard to send towing trucks to the exact "Hidden Violations" that are hurting the city the most.

Key Findings

14 Hidden Violation junctions identified receiving zero targeted enforcement — the biggest opportunity for impact.
Scooters = 56% of violations, only 12% of congestion impact. Our EPS score reweights enforcement away from scooters toward LGVs and trucks that actually block carriageways.
Trained Jan–Apr, validated blind on May: EPS predictions strongly correlate with unseen future congestion — proving the model generalizes to real-world deployment.

What this means: The Enforcement Priority Score (EPS) calculates the exact disruption caused (0-100). A higher score means this parking violation is actively choking a major carriageway, blocking transit, or creating severe delays. Why it matters: Sending a tow truck here instantly clears a major city bottleneck, whereas ticketing a low-score zone does little to help traffic.
